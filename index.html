Landscape

Competitor Analysis

We studied 5 competing platforms to understand what users are gravitating toward — and why.

Reddit

  • Community-driven forum with topic-specific subreddits
  • Strong moderation tools give users control
  • Threaded discussions promote deeper conversation

Threads

  • Text-based social platform by Meta
  • Seamless Instagram integration for onboarding
  • Cleaner, ad-light feed focused on conversation

Mastodon

  • Decentralized, open-source social network
  • Chronological feed with no algorithm
  • User-owned servers give full data control

Bluesky

  • Open protocol with user-controlled algorithms
  • Custom feeds let users curate their experience
  • Transparent moderation builds trust

Discord

  • Server-based communities with rich media
  • Voice, video, and live interaction built in
  • Role-based permissions for effective moderation

Persona

Persona 1

Sporty Sarah

Experienced User

27 · Sports Marketing · Chicago, IL

Interests: Sports, Pop Culture, Politics

Bio

Sarah was born and raised in Chicago, and attended UIUC for college. She loves everything sports-related, and works in Social Media marketing for the Chicago Cubs. She lives a fast-paced lifestyle and is very active online. She likes staying caught up on all current news, no matter the topic. She creates posts on X for work, but also loves to use it for personal use.

Pain Points

  • Too many unnecessary or confusing features
  • Grok/AI everywhere
  • Doesn't understand difference between lists and communities

Needs

  • Easy to quickly post
  • Many options for engagement with fans
  • Ability to search and find what she's looking for

Persona 2

Normal Nora

Social User

30 · Market Analyst · Stockholm

Most Used Apps: X, Instagram, Reddit & WhatsApp

Hobbies: DJ Mixing, Painting & Photography

Bio

Normal Nora is a social human being, she likes to spend time with family and friends. Instagram is a great place for her, but it has a different use case when compared to 'X'. She feels that 'X' must allow friends to share textual ideas with each other and publicly, which is not a major selling point of Instagram.

Pain Points

  • The feed does not have a specific structure
  • Filtering in search lacks options
  • The messages tab is not very intuitive

Needs

  • Customisable feed with options to include specific interests/profiles
  • Search filter updated with options to include time, popularity and other factors
  • A friends tab in the feed would be a useful addition
